Grace and Freedom Test Corpus
==============================

This file is designed for testing multi-level retrieval system.
Each section targets specific retrieval levels.

=== SECTION 1: KEYWORD COMBINATIONS (Level 0 targets) ===

Grace unlocks true freedom in Christ.
When grace unlocks your heart, you experience freedom.
Divine grace unlocks the door to spiritual freedom.
Through grace, freedom becomes possible for all believers.
Grace and freedom are interconnected gifts from God.

=== SECTION 2: SINGLE KEYWORDS (Level 1 targets) ===

Grace comes from God alone.
Grace is freely given to humanity.
The concept of grace transforms lives.
Freedom is a precious gift.
Freedom liberates the soul.
True freedom comes from within.

=== SECTION 3: SYNONYMS (Level 2 targets) ===
Keywords: grace->mercy, freedom->deliverance/liberation

God's mercy brings deliverance to the broken.
Divine mercy flows abundantly to all.
Mercy is the expression of God's love.
Deliverance from bondage is promised to believers.
Liberation comes through accepting God's mercy.
Spiritual liberation transforms the heart.

=== SECTION 4: KEYWORD + MAGICAL WORDS (Level 3 targets) ===
Pattern: "keyword is/brings/leads to..."

Grace is God's unearned favor to humanity.
Grace is the foundation of salvation.
Grace brings transformation to the soul.
Grace leads to eternal life.
Freedom is living without spiritual chains.
Freedom is the birthright of believers.
Freedom brings joy and peace.
Freedom leads to authentic worship.

=== SECTION 5: DEEPER THEOLOGICAL CONTENT ===

The mystery of grace reveals God's character.
Understanding grace requires humility and faith.
Freedom in Christ differs from worldly freedom.
Spiritual freedom enables true worship.
Grace empowers believers to overcome sin.
Freedom from guilt comes through accepting grace.
